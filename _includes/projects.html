HOW IT WORKS

Build. Train. Deploy.

The Intelligent Energy Solutions will automatically compiles energy infrastructure engineering data from various sensors into machine learning models, enabling enterprises to more efficiently build intelligence into real-world systems.

Build

Infrastructure engineering data from various sensors combined with both domain expert knowledge and physics models are fed into the Intelligent Energy Solutions Engine for training.

Learn more

Train

Based on the program, the Intelligent Energy Solutions Engine automatically selects the best machine learning algorithm to train an AI model, with some tuning of the hyperparameters if required.

Learn more

Deploy

A trained model can then stream predictions or recommendations to enterprise applications, drastically improving the operations of their real-world systems. Each model can be continually managed, tuned, debugged, and improved upon from within the Intelligent Energy Solutions Platform.
